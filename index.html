Before You Start

What does the £2 plan include?

£2 is the starting price of our Starter plan, per user per month — not a limited-time trial. It includes access to the Stack; more plans with additional storage, seats and premium apps are on the way. You’ll always see the price before anything changes.

Do I have to use all 27 apps?

No. Every app is included in the Stack, but your team only needs to switch on what it actually uses. Turn apps on or off per user from the admin console.

Can I move my existing email and files over?

Yes. Snaarp Mail and Work Drive both include guided migration from Gmail, Outlook and most common storage providers.

What’s the difference between Books and Accounting Software?

Books covers everyday invoicing and bookkeeping. Accounting Software adds full double-entry accounting and financial reporting for teams that have outgrown the basics.

Is this suitable for a team of one?

Yes — solo founders use the Stack to replace half a dozen separate subscriptions with one. Pricing scales down with your team size.
