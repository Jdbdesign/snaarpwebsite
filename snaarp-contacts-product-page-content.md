## 5. Feature grid (4 cards, matches homepage's 3–5 card pattern)

Each card: icon, title, one-line description.

1. **Auto-sync across the Stack**
   Update a contact once — Mail, Meet, and CRM update instantly, everywhere that contact appears.

2. **Smart dedupe**
   Contacts merges duplicate entries automatically when the same email or phone number shows up twice.

3. **Tags & groups**
   Organize by team, client, or project — filter and find people in seconds, not scrolls.

4. **Search everywhere**
   Find a contact by name, company, tag, or even a note you left on their card — search works across every field.

## 10. FAQ accordion

- **Eyebrow:** QUESTIONS
- **Headline:** Contacts, answered.

1. **Do I have to manually add contacts to each app?** No — save once in Contacts, or let Snaarp save them automatically from an email or meeting, and it syncs everywhere.
2. **Can I import my existing contacts?** Yes — import from Gmail, Outlook, or a CSV file in a couple of clicks.
3. **What happens to duplicate contacts?** Contacts detects matching emails or phone numbers and merges them automatically, with a review step before anything's combined.
4. **Can I organize contacts by team or client?** Yes — use tags and groups to filter your list however works for your team.
5. **Is Contacts included in the £2 Starter plan?** Yes — Contacts is included in every plan, no add-on required.
