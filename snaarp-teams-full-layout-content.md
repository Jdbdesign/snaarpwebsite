# Snaarp — Teams (Full Layout, SuperCRM-style Reference) Content

**Purpose:** full section-by-section copy for the Teams product page, rebuilt as a full multi-section landing experience (same reference structure used for the earlier Contact page draft), restyled entirely in Snaarp's brand system. If approved, this becomes the template pattern for Contacts and the rest of the product pages.

**Reference used:** SuperCRM landing page — layout structure, section rhythm, card patterns, and pacing are borrowed. All copy, colors, and imagery direction below are original to Snaarp.

---

## 0. Style direction (professional, clean, spacious — not cluttered)

- **Primary:** violet `#7C3AED`
- **Accents:** mint, amber, rose, teal — used sparingly as small decorative blobs/dots in section backgrounds, never as primary card color
- **Backgrounds:** alternate white and very light gray `#F7F7F7` between sections for rhythm, same as the reference
- **Shadows:** soft, layered, low-opacity (e.g. two stacked shadows — a tight low-blur one for definition + a wide soft one for lift), never harsh drop shadows
- **Radius:** consistent scale across cards (e.g. 16px cards, 999px pills)
- **Whitespace:** generous section padding (matches the reference's airy spacing), don't crowd cards
- **Imagery:** real, warm, professional stock photography for people/team moments (photo strip, collage) — diverse, natural, not stock-photo-cheesy. Screens/mockups are clean UI renders, not photos.

## 12. FAQ accordion

- **Eyebrow:** QUESTIONS
- **Headline:** Teams, answered.

1. **How is this different from Meet?** Teams is for chat, channels, and file sharing. Meet is Snaarp's separate video calling app — jump from a channel straight into a Meet call.
2. **Is there a limit on channels or message history?** No — unlimited channels and unlimited searchable history on every plan.
3. **Can I make a channel private?** Yes — public or invite-only.
4. **Do files stay attached to the conversation?** Yes — anything shared in a channel stays linked to that thread.
5. **Is Teams included in the £1 Starter plan?** Yes — included in every plan, no add-on required.
