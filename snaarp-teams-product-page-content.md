## 5. Feature grid (4 cards)

1. **Channels & threads**
   Create a channel per project or team, and keep side conversations in threads instead of cluttering the main feed.

2. **File sharing, built in**
   Drop a file into a channel and it stays attached to that conversation — searchable later, no digging through old messages.

3. **@Mentions & notifications**
   Tag a person or a whole channel, and control exactly what you get notified about, channel by channel.

4. **Search everything**
   Find a message, a file, or a decision from six months ago — search covers every channel and thread you're part of.

---

## 6. How it works (3 steps)

1. **Create a channel**
   Set one up for a project, a team, or a topic — public for anyone to join, or private for just the people who need it.
2. **Start talking**
   Post updates, share files, and thread replies so side conversations don't clutter the main channel.
3. **Everyone stays in sync**
   New teammates can scroll back through channel history to get context instantly — nothing's siloed in someone's inbox.

## 10. FAQ accordion

- **Eyebrow:** QUESTIONS
- **Headline:** Teams, answered.

1. **How is this different from Meet?** Teams is for chat, channels, and file sharing. Meet is Snaarp's separate video calling app — you can jump from a Teams channel straight into a Meet call.
2. **Is there a limit on channels or message history?** No — unlimited channels and unlimited searchable history on every plan.
3. **Can I make a channel private?** Yes — channels can be public (open to anyone on the team) or private (invite-only).
4. **Do files stay attached to the conversation?** Yes — anything shared in a channel stays linked to that thread, and is searchable later.
5. **Is Teams included in the £1 Starter plan?** Yes — Teams is included in every plan, no add-on required.
